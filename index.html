About us

Dominion Petrogas is the General Partner of the Maxximus Royalty Investment Fund

Our funds are structured under Regulation S and are designed specifically for non-U.S. investors. Maxximus Royalty, L.P. is dedicated to buying Oil & Gas Mineral Rights (Reserves) mainly in the Western Gulf Basin in South Texas (Eagle Ford Formation), Williston Basin in North Dakota (Bakken Formation), Texas Louisiana Salt Basin (Haynesville Formation) and Permian Basin in West Texas (several formations). Our strategy is about diversifying the risk through the investment of our funds in Oil and Gas in several properties managed by different operators. We are currently completing a $30 million dollar round for Maxximus Royalty to invest in Mineral Rights Reserves. Our investors can participate by buying notes through their existing Brokers! Our target annual rate offered to our investors is 14%.

Mission

Vision

Strategic Partnership

We partnered with Arkoma International, LLC, one of the most reputable Minerals acquisition companies in the United States. Arkoma has bought Oil & Gas Mineral Interests since 1983.

Maxximus Royalty, L.P. invests in the following type of Mineral prospects:

Producing Properties

We invest in units that are already producing hydrocarbons and generating cash flow with room to grow.

Shale Plays

We acquire Mineral interests in units where horizontal wells are developed and produced.

Upside

Our investments in Minerals are directed to opportunities where we the acquisition price secures a return for our investors, more wells will be drilled in our properties, there are several formations where hydrocarbons may be produced and new technology will increase the production in specific formations.

Reputable Operators

We only invest where the unit is operated by financially healthy companies with a solid balance sheet.
