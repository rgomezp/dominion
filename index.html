About us

General Partner of the Maxximus Royalty and Maxximus Oil & Gas investment funds

Our funds are designed specifically for non-U.S. investors under Regulation S. Maxximus Royalty, L.P. is our first fund, dedicated to buy Oil & Gas Mineral Rights mainly in the Western Gulf Basin in South Texas (Eagle Ford Formation), Williston Basin in North Dakota (Bakken Formation), Texas Louisiana Salt Basin (Haynesville Formation) and Permian Basin in West Texas (several formations). Our strategy is about diversifying the risk through the investment of our funds in Oil and Gas in several properties and managed by different operators . We are currently completing a $30 million dollar round for Maxximus Royalty to invest in Mineral Rights. Our investors can participate by buying notes through their existing Brokers!

Mission

“To produce the highest return for our Investors through the intelligent investment of our Funds’ resources in Oil & Gas Minerals and Wells Development in the United States”

Vision

“To manage one of the largest, most profitable and most trustable U.S. small-cap Oil and Gas Funds catered to foreign investors"

Strategic Partnership

We partnered with Arkoma International, LLC, one of the most reputable Minerals acquisition companies in the United States. Arkoma has bought Oil & Gas Mineral Interests since 1983.
